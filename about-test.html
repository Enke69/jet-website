Why Choose IELTS

What can IELTS do for you?

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is the world's most popular English language proficiency test for higher education and global migration. It is trusted by over 11,000 organizations worldwide including universities, employers, governments, and immigration authorities.

🎓 Study Abroad

IELTS is accepted by universities in over 140 countries, including the UK, USA, Australia, Canada, and New Zealand.

🌍 Migrate

IELTS is a requirement for immigration to Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom.

💼 Work Internationally

Many employers and professional registration bodies require proof of your English skills through IELTS.

📈 Career Growth

A strong IELTS score can open doors to international opportunities and career advancement.

Test Types

IELTS offers two types of tests. Choose the one that's right for your goals:

🎓 IELTS Academic

  • For students applying to higher education
  • For professional registration (doctors, nurses, etc.)
  • Tests academic reading and writing skills
  • Uses academic texts and topics
  • Accepted by all universities worldwide
  • Most common IELTS test type

🌍 IELTS General Training

  • For immigration to English-speaking countries
  • For work experience or training programs
  • For secondary education applications
  • Uses everyday English texts and topics
  • Required by immigration authorities
  • Writing tasks focus on real-life scenarios

Test Format

The IELTS test consists of four sections. Here's what to expect:

Section Duration Details
Listening 30 minutes 4 recordings of native English speakers, 40 questions
Reading 60 minutes 3 reading passages with 40 questions
Writing 60 minutes 2 tasks: Task 1 (150 words) and Task 2 (250 words)
Speaking 11–14 minutes 3 parts: introduction, long turn, and discussion

⏱️ Total Test Time

The Listening, Reading, and Writing sections are completed on the same day with no breaks. The Speaking test may be taken on the same day or within 7 days before or after the other tests.

OSR & EOR

The One Skill Retake allows you to retake just one section of the IELTS test (Listening, Reading, Writing, or Speaking) if you didn't get the score you needed. This saves you time and money compared to retaking the entire test.

  • Available for computer-delivered IELTS only
  • Must be taken within 60 days of your original test
  • Your best score from each section is combined for a new overall band score

If you believe your IELTS test was not scored accurately, you can request an Enquiry on Results (EOR), which is a re-marking of your test.

  • Can be requested for one or all test components
  • Must be requested within 6 weeks of your test date
  • Fee: 420,000 MNT (refunded if your score changes)
  • Results usually available within 2–3 weeks
