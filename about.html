Our Centre

JET Institute is located in the heart of Ulaanbaatar at Max Centre, 5th Floor. Our modern facilities are designed to provide the best possible learning and testing environment for IELTS candidates.

🖥️ Computer Labs

Modern computer-delivered IELTS testing rooms with individual workstations and noise-cancelling headphones.

📚 Study Areas

Quiet study spaces and a resource library with IELTS preparation materials available to all students.

🏢 Central Location

Conveniently located at Max Centre, 5th Floor in the Sukhbaatar District, easily accessible by public transport.

☕ Student Lounge

Comfortable common areas where students can relax, network, and practice their English conversation skills.

Our Policy

We are committed to providing transparent, fair, and professional services to all our students and test takers.

📋 Test Day Policy

  • Arrive at least 30 minutes before your scheduled time
  • Bring a valid, original passport or national ID
  • No electronic devices allowed in the testing room
  • Water in transparent bottles only
  • Late arrivals may not be permitted to take the test

💰 Refund & Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations must be made 5+ weeks before test date
  • Cancellation fee: 230,000 MNT (deducted from refund)
  • Test date transfers cost 230,000 MNT
  • No refund for no-shows or less than 5 weeks notice
  • Medical exceptions may apply with documentation

🔒 Data Privacy Policy

  • Personal information is kept strictly confidential
  • Data is shared only with British Council for test administration
  • Test results are sent only to designated organizations
  • We comply with Mongolia's data protection regulations

♿ Accessibility Policy

  • Special arrangements available for test takers with disabilities
  • Requests must be made at time of registration
  • Medical evidence may be required
  • Contact us for more information about available accommodations
