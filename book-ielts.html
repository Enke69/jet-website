Book Your IELTS Test

Register, pay, and get ready for your test at Mongolia's official IELTS Test Centre.

How To Register

Registration Steps

Follow these simple steps to book your IELTS test.

1

Register Online

Create an account and register at the British Council IELTS portal. Select JET Institute as your test centre.

2

Transfer Payment

Transfer the test fee of 899,000 MNT to one of our bank accounts listed below.

3

Send Confirmation

Email your payment proof, reference number, and name to ielts.jetschool@gmail.com within 24 hours.

4

Receive Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email with your test date, time, and venue details.
