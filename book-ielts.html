How To Register

Registration Steps

Follow these simple steps to book your IELTS test.

1

Register Online

Create an account and register at the British Council IELTS portal. Select JET Institute as your test centre.

2

Transfer Payment

Transfer the test fee of 899,000 MNT to one of our bank accounts listed below.

3

Send Confirmation

Email your payment proof, reference number, and name to ielts.jetschool@gmail.com within 24 hours.

4

Receive Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation email with your test date, time, and venue details.

TRF Delivery

Your Test Report Form (TRF) will be available for collection at JET Institute

🖨️ TRF Print & Collection

Your official Test Report Form (TRF) can be collected from JET Institute 13 days after your test. Please bring your valid passport or national ID card for verification.

You can also request up to 5 additional copies of your TRF to be sent to organizations. Extra TRF copies cost 40,000 MNT each.
