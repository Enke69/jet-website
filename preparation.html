Expert Advice

IELTS Preparation Tips

Proven strategies from our experienced instructors to help you succeed.

🎧

Listening

Practice with various English accents. Focus on keywords and anticipate answers before they come.

📖

Reading

Skim and scan techniques save time. Practice reading academic texts and identifying main ideas quickly.

✍️

Writing

Plan your essays before writing. Use clear paragraph structure and varied vocabulary. Always proofread.

🗣️

Speaking

Speak English daily. Record yourself to identify areas for improvement. Expand your answers naturally.
